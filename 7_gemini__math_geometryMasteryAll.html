📚 Mathematical Reference Guide

Pythagorean & Trigonometry

• Pythagorean: a² + b² = c²
• sin = Opposite / Hypotenuse
• cos = Adjacent / Hypotenuse
• tan = Opposite / Adjacent
• 45-45-90: Hyp = Leg × √2
• 30-60-90: Sides = x, x√3, 2x

Coordinate Geometry

• Distance: d = √((x₂−x₁)² + (y₂−y₁)²)
• Midpoint: M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)
• Slope: m = (y₂−y₁) / (x₂−x₁)
• Shoelace Area:
  ½|x₁(y₂−y₃)+x₂(y₃−y₁)+x₃(y₁−y₂)|

2D Areas

• Triangle: A = ½ × b × h
• Trapezoid: A = ½ × (a + b) × h
• Rhombus: A = ½ × d₁ × d₂
• Parallelogram: A = b × h
• Annulus: A = π(R² − r²)
• Similar Figures: a₁/a₂ = b₁/b₂

Angles

• Interior Sum: (n − 2) × 180°
• Each Interior (regular): (n−2)×180° / n
• Exterior Angle (tri): = sum of 2 remote interior
• Transversal: Alternate interior = equal
• Co-interior (same-side): sum = 180°
• Corresponding angles: equal

Circles, Arcs & Sectors

• Area: A = πr²  |  Circum: C = 2πr
• Sector Area: (θ/360) × πr²
• Arc Length: (θ/360) × 2πr
• Semicircle Area: ½πr²
• Annulus: π(R² − r²)
• π ≈ 3.14 or 22/7

3D Volume & Surface Area

• Prism V: Base Area × h
• Cylinder V: πr²h   SA: 2πr² + 2πrh
• Cone V: ⅓πr²h
• Pyramid V: ⅓ × Base Area × h
• Sphere V: ⁴⁄₃πr³   SA: 4πr²

Transformations

• Reflection x-axis: (x, y) → (x, −y)
• Reflection y-axis: (x, y) → (−x, y)
• Rotation 90° CW: (x, y) → (y, −x)
• Rotation 180°: (x, y) → (−x, −y)
• Dilation: (x, y) → (kx, ky)

Study Guide: Key Formulas

Right Triangle: a² + b² = c² | Area = ½bh
Rectangle: Area = l × w | Perimeter = 2(l + w)
Circle: Area = πr² | Circumf. = 2πr (π ≈ 3.14)
Cylinder: Volume = πr²h | CSA = 2πrh
Sphere: Volume = 4/3πr³ | SA = 4πr²
Trapezoid: Area = ½(a + b)h
Regular Polygon: Angle Sum = (n-2) × 180°
Rect. Prism: Vol = lwh | SA = 2(lw + lh + wh)

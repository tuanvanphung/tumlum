Geometry with Multiple Concepts Practice

Key Formulas

  • Pythagorean Theorem: a² + b² = c²
  • Area of Triangle: 12 × base × height
  • Area of Equilateral Triangle: √34 s²
  • Area of Square: s²
  • Perimeter of Square: 4s
  • Area of Rectangle: Length × Width (L × W)
  • Perimeter of Rectangle: 2(L + W)
  • Area of Circle: πr²
  • Circumference of Circle: 2πr or πd
  • Area of Semicircle: 12 πr²
  • Arc Length: (θ360°) × 2πr
  • Area of Sector: (θ360°) × πr²
  • Surface Area of Cuboid: 2(LW + LH + WH)
  • Volume of Cuboid: L × W × H
  • Surface Area of Cube: 6s²
  • Volume of Cube: s³
  • Curved Surface Area of Cylinder: 2πrh
  • Total Surface Area of Cylinder: 2πrh + 2πr²
  • Volume of Cylinder: πr²h
  • Curved Surface Area of Cone: πrl (l = slant height)
  • Volume of Cone: 13 πr²h
  • Surface Area of Sphere: 4πr²
  • Volume of Sphere: 43 πr³
  • Curved Surface Area of Hemisphere: 2πr²
  • Total Surface Area of Hemisphere: 3πr²
  • Volume of Hemisphere: 23 πr³
  • Area of Parallelogram: base × height
  • Perimeter of Parallelogram: 2(a + b)
  • Area of Rhombus: 12 d₁d₂
  • Area of Trapezoid: 12 (a+b)h
  • Sum of Interior Angles of Polygon: (n-2) × 180°
  • Exterior Angle of Regular Polygon: 360°n
  • Space Diagonal of Cuboid: √(L² + W² + H²)
  • Distance Formula: √((x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)² )
  • Density: MassVolume
  • Scale Factors: Linear (k), Area (k²), Volume (k³)

Note: Use π ≈ 3.14 or π ≈ 22/7 as indicated in specific problems.

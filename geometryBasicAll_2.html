Mathematical Reference Guide

Angles & Lines

• Supplementary: Line Sum = 180°
• Complementary: Right Sum = 90°
• Triangle Sum: Interior Sum = 180°
• Parallel Lines: Alt. Interior Angles are equal

2D Area Formulas

• Triangle: Area = ½ × b × h
• Parallelogram: Area = b × h
• Trapezoid: Area = ½(a + b)h
• Rhombus: Area = ½ × d₁ × d₂

Circles & Scaling

• Circle: A = πr² | C = 2πr (π ≈ 3.14)
• Distance: √((x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²)
• Scale Factor: New side = Old side × k
• Rounds: Total Distance = Perimeter × Rounds

3D Volume & Area

• Cylinder: V = πr²h | CSA = 2πrh
• Cone: V = ⅓πr²h | CSA = πrl
• Sphere: SA = 4πr²
• Prism: V = lwh | SA = 2(lw+lh+wh)

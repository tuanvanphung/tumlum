Mathematical Reference Guide

Theorems & Trigonometry

• Pythagoras: a² + b² = c²
• Distance: d = √((x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²)
• Trig: sin(θ)=O/H | cos(θ)=A/H | tan(θ)=O/A
• Special 45-45-90: Hypotenuse = Side × √2

2D Measurement

• Triangle Area: ½ × b × h
• Trapezoid Area: ½ × (a + b) × h
• Rhombus Area: ½ × d₁ × d₂
• Polygon Interior Sum: (n - 2) × 180°
• Similar Triangles: a₁/a₂ = b₁/b₂

Circles & Sectors

• Circle: A = πr² | C = 2πr (π ≈ 3.14)
• Sector Area: (θ/360) × πr²
• Arc Length: (θ/360) × 2πr
• Exact Calculation: π ≈ 22/7

3D Volume & Surface Area

• Prism/Cylinder Vol: V = Area_Base × h
• Pyramid/Cone Vol: V = ⅓ × Area_Base × h
• Sphere: V = 4/3πr³ | SA = 4πr²
• Cylinder SA: 2πr² + 2πrh
